Retaining Walls Built for Commercial Properties

Commercial retaining walls must handle significant earth pressures, meet building codes, and withstand decades of service. Whether you need new wall construction to manage grade changes on a development site or repair of failing existing walls, the stakes are high on commercial projects.

FCS designs and builds commercial retaining walls using engineered solutions appropriate for the soil conditions and load requirements of your specific project. We work with your architects and civil engineers to deliver walls that perform structurally while meeting aesthetic requirements.

Common Questions

Commercial Retaining Wall Services FAQs

Do commercial retaining walls need engineering?−
Yes. Most commercial retaining walls require engineering design to meet building codes and ensure structural adequacy. Walls over 4 feet typically require permits and engineered drawings. FCS works with structural engineers to design walls that meet all requirements.
How long do commercial retaining walls last?+
Properly designed and constructed commercial retaining walls should last 50+ years. Key factors include proper drainage behind the wall, adequate reinforcement, and quality materials. Our walls are built to commercial standards with long-term performance in mind.
Can you repair a leaning retaining wall?+
Often, yes. Depending on the cause and extent of failure, we can repair leaning walls using soil anchors, tiebacks, or partial rebuilds. In some cases, full replacement is more cost-effective. We'll evaluate your wall and recommend the best approach.
Do you handle retaining walls on new construction sites?+
Yes. We provide retaining wall construction for new commercial developments, including site grading walls, building pad walls, and parking lot retaining structures. We coordinate with your general contractor and civil engineer.
How much do commercial retaining walls cost?+
Commercial retaining wall costs depend on wall type, height, length, and site conditions. Typical costs range from $25-$75 per square face foot for engineered block walls. Complex projects with significant height or poor soil conditions will be higher. We provide detailed proposals after site evaluation.
