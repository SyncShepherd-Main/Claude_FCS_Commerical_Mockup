Common Questions

Commercial Excavation Services FAQs

Do you handle utility locating before excavation?−
Yes. We coordinate with utility locating services before any excavation work begins. This is critical for safety and to avoid damage to underground utilities. We follow all 811 requirements and work with local utility companies.
Can you excavate in tight commercial spaces?+
Yes. We have equipment ranging from compact mini-excavators for tight spaces to full-size equipment for large sites. Our operators are experienced in working around existing structures, utilities, and active commercial operations.
Do you provide compaction testing?+
We can coordinate with geotechnical testing firms for compaction testing when required by your project specifications. Proper compaction is critical for foundation performance and we ensure all backfill meets engineering requirements.
What soil types do you encounter in Dallas-Fort Worth?+
DFW has a variety of soil types, but expansive clay is the most common and challenging. This clay expands significantly when wet and shrinks when dry, which is the primary cause of foundation problems in the area. We understand how to work with these soils effectively.
Can excavation work be done near occupied buildings?+
Yes. We regularly perform excavation adjacent to occupied commercial buildings. We use appropriate equipment for the space, implement vibration monitoring when needed, and take precautions to protect existing structures and utilities.
