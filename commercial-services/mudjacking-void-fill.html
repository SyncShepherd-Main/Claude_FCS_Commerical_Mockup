Slab Leveling & Void Fill for Commercial Properties

Settled slabs, uneven floors, and voids beneath commercial structures create safety hazards, ADA compliance issues, and operational problems. Mudjacking and void fill solutions offer a fast, cost-effective way to restore level surfaces without the expense of full slab replacement.

FCS provides commercial mudjacking and void fill services that address settled sidewalks, parking lots, warehouse floors, loading docks, and building slabs. Our methods are minimally invasive, often completed in hours rather than days, and allow your property to return to normal operations quickly.

Common Questions

Commercial Mudjacking & Void Fill FAQs

What's the difference between mudjacking and foundation repair?−
Mudjacking lifts and levels concrete slabs that have settled, while foundation repair addresses structural issues with the foundation itself using piers driven to load-bearing soil. Sometimes both services are needed—we'll recommend the right solution after evaluating your property.
How quickly can mudjacking be completed?+
Most commercial mudjacking projects can be completed in 1-3 days depending on scope. The concrete is typically ready for light traffic within 24 hours. This makes mudjacking ideal for commercial properties that need fast turnaround.
Is mudjacking a permanent solution?+
Mudjacking provides long-lasting results, especially when combined with proper drainage. For situations where ongoing settlement is caused by deeper soil issues, we may recommend foundation piers for a permanent solution. We'll give you an honest assessment of which approach is best for your property.
Can you level warehouse floors for racking systems?+
Yes. We perform precision leveling for warehouse and industrial floors where racking systems require specific flatness tolerances. We can work in phases to avoid disrupting your operations.
How much does commercial mudjacking cost?+
Commercial mudjacking typically costs $3-$8 per square foot, depending on the extent of settlement and accessibility. This is significantly less expensive than slab replacement. We provide exact pricing after our free evaluation.
