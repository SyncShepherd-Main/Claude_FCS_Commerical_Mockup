Questions?

Commercial FAQs

What types of commercial properties do you serve? −
We serve a wide range of commercial properties including multi-family (apartments, townhomes, condos, senior living), office buildings, industrial facilities (warehouses, manufacturing), medical offices and clinics, and we partner with home builders for new construction and warranty repairs.
How much does commercial foundation repair cost? +
Commercial projects vary significantly based on building size, foundation type, and scope of work. We provide free, detailed estimates with no obligation. Our team will assess your specific situation and provide a transparent quote that includes all costs upfront.
Will repairs disrupt my tenants or business operations? +
We specialize in minimizing disruption. We can work in phases, coordinate around tenant schedules, and offer flexible scheduling including nights and weekends. For multi-family properties, we communicate directly with affected residents to ensure smooth operations throughout the project.
Do you provide documentation for insurance or property sales? +
Yes, we provide comprehensive documentation including detailed inspection reports, before/after photos, engineering specifications, and warranty certificates. Our documentation is designed to meet the requirements of insurance companies, investors, lenders, and property transaction stakeholders.
What is your warranty on commercial work? +
We offer a Lifetime Transferable Warranty on our foundation repair work. This warranty stays with the property if ownership changes, providing long-term protection and added value for property transactions. Specific warranty terms vary by service type.
Do you offer financing for commercial projects? +
Yes, we offer flexible financing options to help manage project costs. We understand that foundation and concrete repairs can be unexpected expenses, and we work with property owners to find payment solutions that fit their budget and cash flow requirements.
