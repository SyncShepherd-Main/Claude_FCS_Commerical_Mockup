FAQs

Concrete Services for Office / Corporate Questions

What concrete thickness is needed for office/corporate properties?−
Thickness depends on the application. Sidewalks need 4-5 inches, parking areas 5-6 inches with reinforcement, and heavy-traffic areas 6-8+ inches. We specify the right thickness for each area of your office/corporate property.
Can concrete work be done while my office/corporate property is occupied?+
Absolutely. We work in phases, completing one section at a time while maintaining access to the rest of the property. We provide temporary signage and after-hours scheduling and professional worksite standards to minimize tenant and visitor impact.
How do you handle ADA requirements for office/corporate properties?+
We ensure all concrete work meets current ADA standards for slopes, cross-slopes, detectable warnings, and accessible routes. This protects your office/corporate property from compliance issues.
