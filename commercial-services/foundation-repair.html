Foundation Repair Engineered for Commercial Properties

Commercial foundation problems don't just affect your building—they affect your tenants, your operations, and your bottom line. Settlement, heaving, and structural movement in commercial properties require solutions engineered for heavier loads, larger footprints, and the complexity of multi-use structures.

FCS has performed commercial foundation repairs across Dallas-Fort Worth for over 25 years. We understand that commercial projects demand a different level of engineering, documentation, and project management than residential work. Our team has the experience and equipment to handle everything from small retail buildings to large multi-story complexes.

Common Questions

Commercial Foundation Repair FAQs

How much does commercial foundation repair cost?−
Commercial foundation repair costs vary widely based on building size, number of piers needed, accessibility, and soil conditions. Small commercial buildings may start around $10,000-$15,000, while large multi-story or industrial projects can range from $50,000 to $200,000+. We provide detailed, transparent proposals after our free evaluation.
How long does commercial foundation repair take?+
Most commercial projects take 2-8 weeks depending on building size and complexity. We develop phased schedules that minimize operational disruption. Single-story buildings may be completed in 1-2 weeks, while large industrial or multi-story projects may take 6-8 weeks.
Will my business need to close during repairs?+
In most cases, no. We schedule work to minimize disruption and can often work around your operations, including evenings and weekends. We'll develop a project plan that keeps your business running throughout the repair process.
Do you provide engineering reports?+
Yes. Every commercial project includes comprehensive documentation including engineering assessments, pier placement drawings, before/after measurements, and warranty certificates. Our reports meet the standards required for commercial real estate transactions and regulatory compliance.
What warranty do you offer on commercial work?+
We offer a Lifetime Transferable Warranty on our foundation repair work. This warranty transfers with the property if sold, adding value to your commercial investment and providing long-term peace of mind for property owners and investors.
