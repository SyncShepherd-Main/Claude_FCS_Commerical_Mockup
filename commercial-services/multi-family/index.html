Foundation & Concrete Experts for Multi-Family Properties

Multi-family properties face unique foundation challenges that single-family homes don't encounter. The combination of shared structural loads, multiple units with varying settlement patterns, and the need to coordinate repairs around dozens or even hundreds of residents requires specialized expertise.

At FCS, we've spent over 25 years developing efficient repair strategies specifically for multi-family properties. We understand that your residents' comfort and your property's reputation depend on minimizing disruption while delivering permanent solutions.

Whether you're a property manager dealing with foundation issues across multiple buildings, an HOA board addressing community-wide concerns, or an investor protecting your portfolio, we have the experience and resources to handle projects of any scale.

Common Questions

Multi-Family FAQs

How do you coordinate repairs with tenants? −
We handle all tenant communication, including advance notice letters, scheduling, and day-of coordination. Our crews are trained to work efficiently and respectfully around residents. Most repairs don't require tenants to leave their units, and when access is needed, we schedule around their availability.
Can foundation repairs be phased across multiple budget years? +
Yes, we frequently work with HOAs and property management companies to phase repairs across budget cycles. We'll help you prioritize which buildings or areas need immediate attention and which can wait, creating a multi-year plan that matches your financial timeline.
Do you provide documentation for HOA boards? +
Absolutely. We provide detailed inspection reports, engineering assessments, photo documentation, and warranty certificates. Our reports are formatted to support reserve study updates, insurance claims, and property transaction due diligence. We can also present findings directly to your board.
What's the typical timeline for a multi-building project? +
Timeline varies based on scope, but most multi-family projects can be completed in 2-8 weeks. We typically complete one building or section at a time to minimize disruption. After your free evaluation, we'll provide a detailed project schedule.
How does your warranty work for multi-family properties? +
Our Lifetime Transferable Warranty covers our foundation repair work and transfers with ownership of the property. This provides long-term protection and adds value during sales or refinancing. Specific terms vary by service type.
Do you work with affordable housing compliance requirements? +
Yes, we have experience with HUD, LIHTC, and Section 8 properties. We understand the documentation and compliance requirements for affordable housing and can provide the paperwork needed for regulatory inspections and audits.
