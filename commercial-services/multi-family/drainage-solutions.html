FAQs

Drainage Solutions for Multi-Family Questions

Why do multi-family properties need special drainage?−
Multi-Family properties have unique drainage challenges including larger roof areas, more impervious surfaces, and multiple buildings that concentrate water flow. Proper drainage prevents the soil expansion/contraction cycle that damages foundations in North Texas.
Can drainage be installed without disrupting multi-family operations?+
Yes. Drainage work is primarily exterior and can be completed in phases across your property. We schedule work to minimize impact on parking, access, and daily operations using phased scheduling and tenant communication support.
How much does commercial drainage cost for multi-family properties?+
Costs depend on property size and system complexity. Basic perimeter drainage for a single building starts around $3,000-$5,000, while comprehensive multi-building systems can range higher. We provide detailed proposals after evaluating your specific situation.
