Excavation Tailored for Multi-Family

Multi-Family properties present unique challenges for excavation services. With apartment communities, townhomes, condominiums, student housing, senior living, and affordable housing properties, property managers and HOA boards need a contractor who understands the complexities of multi-family excavation — especially coordinating work across multiple units while maintaining tenant satisfaction.

We coordinate utility locating before any excavation begins, then execute precise excavation using equipment sized appropriately for your site. All backfill is compacted to engineering specifications. We restore the site to pre-work conditions and coordinate with your team for phased scheduling and tenant communication support.

FAQs

Excavation for Multi-Family Questions

Can you excavate near occupied multi-family buildings?−
Yes. We regularly perform excavation adjacent to occupied multi-family buildings using appropriately sized equipment, vibration precautions, and safety protocols to protect existing structures and occupants.
Do you handle utility locating for multi-family projects?+
Yes. We coordinate with 811 and utility companies before any excavation. This is critical for safety, especially on multi-family properties with complex underground utilities.
What soil types do you encounter at multi-family sites in DFW?+
Dallas-Fort Worth primarily has expansive clay soil that swells when wet and shrinks when dry. This is the primary cause of foundation problems. We understand how to work with these soils effectively on multi-family projects.
