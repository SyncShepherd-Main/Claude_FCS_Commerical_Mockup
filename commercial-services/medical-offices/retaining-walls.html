FAQs

Retaining Walls for Medical Offices Questions

Do retaining walls at medical offices properties require engineering?−
Walls over 4 feet typically require engineered drawings and permits. FCS works with structural engineers to design walls that meet all code requirements for medical offices applications.
Can you repair a failing retaining wall on my medical offices property?+
Often, yes. Depending on the failure cause, we can repair using soil anchors, tiebacks, or partial rebuilds. We'll evaluate the wall and recommend the most cost-effective solution.
How long do commercial retaining walls last?+
Properly designed and constructed walls last 50+ years. Key factors include proper drainage behind the wall, adequate reinforcement, and quality materials suited to medical offices property demands.
