Common Questions

Medical Office FAQs

Will repairs affect our medical equipment?−
We coordinate with your equipment vendors before any work begins. For sensitive equipment like MRI and CT scanners, we develop repair plans that minimize vibration and movement. In most cases, equipment recalibration needs are minimal or unnecessary.
Can you work around our patient schedule?+
Absolutely. We routinely schedule work during evenings, weekends, and between patient appointments. We can also work in phases, completing one area at a time while you maintain operations in other parts of the facility.
How do you maintain cleanliness standards?+
Our crews follow healthcare-appropriate protocols including dust containment, clean work areas, and proper waste disposal. We understand the importance of maintaining a sterile environment and take extra precautions in medical facilities.
Do you provide compliance documentation?+
Yes, we provide comprehensive documentation including engineering reports, before/after measurements, ADA compliance verification, and warranty certificates. Our reports are formatted to support OSHA compliance, accreditation requirements, and facility audits.
Can foundation issues affect ADA compliance?+
Yes, foundation settlement can create uneven floors, door alignment issues, and accessibility problems. Our repairs restore proper floor levels and door operation, helping you maintain ADA compliance throughout your facility.
