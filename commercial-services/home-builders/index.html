Common Questions

Builder FAQs

Do you offer volume pricing for builders?−
Yes, we offer competitive volume pricing for builder partners with ongoing projects. Our builder program includes preferential scheduling, dedicated points of contact, and streamlined billing. Contact us to discuss partnership terms for your communities.
How quickly can you respond to warranty claims?+
For builder partners, we target a 48-hour response time for warranty evaluations. We understand that fast response protects your reputation with homebuyers and helps manage warranty costs. Emergency situations receive same-day attention.
Can you handle multiple communities simultaneously?+
Absolutely. We have the crew capacity and equipment to service multiple builder communities at the same time. Your dedicated builder liaison coordinates scheduling across all your active projects.
What services can you provide during new construction?+
We provide the full range of site services: excavation and grading, drainage system installation, retaining wall construction, concrete flatwork (driveways, walkways, patios), and foundation evaluation/repair when needed. We can be your single-source partner for all below-grade and concrete work.
How does your warranty benefit our homebuyers?+
Our Lifetime Transferable Warranty gives your homebuyers confidence in their foundation and adds value to the home. It transfers with the property if they sell, which is a selling point you can promote. This warranty also reduces your long-term warranty exposure.
