Foundation & Concrete Built for Industrial Demands

Industrial facilities face foundation challenges that no other property type encounters. The combination of extreme static loads from stored inventory, dynamic loads from forklifts and heavy equipment, and expansive concrete slabs creates unique stresses that require specialized engineering and repair techniques.

At FCS, we've developed expertise specifically for industrial properties over 25+ years. We understand that warehouse and manufacturing operations can't simply shut down for repairs—your supply chain and production schedules depend on keeping your facility operational.

Whether you're a facility manager dealing with floor settlement affecting racking systems, a logistics director concerned about loading dock issues, or an investor protecting your industrial portfolio, we have the heavy-duty solutions your facility requires.

Common Questions

Industrial FAQs

Can you work around our 24/7 operations?−
Absolutely. We specialize in working around continuous operations. We'll develop a repair plan that addresses critical areas during shift changes, weekends, or low-volume periods. Many of our industrial projects are completed without any operational downtime.
How do you handle heavy racking and inventory during repairs?+
We work in phases, addressing one area at a time while you maintain operations in other zones. In most cases, racking can remain loaded during repairs. We'll coordinate with your team to temporarily relocate inventory only when absolutely necessary.
Can you repair loading docks without disrupting shipping?+
Yes. We can repair loading docks one at a time, typically completing each dock in 1-2 days. We schedule dock repairs to minimize impact on your shipping schedule and can work nights and weekends if needed.
What about floor flatness for automated systems?+
We understand the precision requirements for automated sorting systems, AGVs, and high-density racking. Our repair methods can restore floor flatness to meet the specifications required by your equipment manufacturers.
Do you provide documentation for facility audits?+
Yes, we provide comprehensive documentation including engineering assessments, repair specifications, before/after measurements, and warranty certificates. Our reports meet the requirements for facility audits, insurance purposes, and property transactions.
