FAQs

Foundation Repair for Industrial Questions

How many piers will my industrial building need?−
Pier count depends on building size, load distribution, and extent of settlement. A typical Distribution Center might need 20-80+ piers. We determine the exact number during our free evaluation using elevation measurements and structural analysis.
Will industrial operations be disrupted during repair?+
We design every project plan around your operations. For industrial properties, that means phased repairs during shift changes and zero-downtime scheduling. Most clients report minimal to no disruption to daily activities.
How long does industrial foundation repair take?+
Timeline depends on building size and pier count. Smaller buildings take 1-2 weeks; larger industrial projects may take 4-8 weeks with phased scheduling to minimize impact on your operations.
