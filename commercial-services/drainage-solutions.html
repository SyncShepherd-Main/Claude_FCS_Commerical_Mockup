Drainage Solutions Designed for Commercial Properties

Water is the #1 enemy of commercial foundations in North Texas. Dallas-Fort Worth's expansive clay soils expand when wet and shrink when dry, creating a cycle of movement that damages foundations over time. Proper drainage isn't just maintenance—it's foundation protection.

FCS designs and installs commercial drainage systems that manage water effectively across large properties, parking lots, building perimeters, and common areas. We understand the unique drainage challenges of commercial properties including higher water volumes, larger impervious surfaces, and regulatory requirements.

Common Questions

Commercial Drainage Solutions FAQs

Why is drainage so important for commercial foundations?−
Dallas-Fort Worth sits on highly expansive clay soil that swells when wet and shrinks when dry. This cycle causes foundation movement. Proper drainage keeps moisture levels consistent around your foundation, preventing the expansion/contraction cycle that causes cracking, settlement, and structural damage.
How do I know if my property has drainage problems?+
Signs include standing water near the building after rain, erosion along the foundation, water stains on exterior walls, soggy landscaping, and foundation movement. If you notice water pooling within 10 feet of your building, you likely have a drainage issue that needs attention.
Can you install drainage without disrupting my tenants?+
Yes. We schedule drainage work to minimize disruption and can work in phases across your property. Most drainage installations are exterior work that doesn't affect interior operations. We restore landscaping and hardscaping to pre-work conditions.
How much do commercial drainage systems cost?+
Commercial drainage costs depend on property size, system type, and complexity. Basic French drain installations start around $3,000-$5,000, while comprehensive property-wide systems for large commercial sites can range from $15,000-$50,000+. We provide detailed proposals after evaluating your specific situation.
Do you handle municipal drainage requirements?+
Yes. Commercial properties often have stormwater management requirements from local municipalities. We design drainage solutions that meet these requirements while also protecting your foundation. We can coordinate with your civil engineer if needed.
